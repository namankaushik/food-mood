Gujarati Handvo

A savory lentil and rice cake, packed with nutrients.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 40 mins

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup mixed lentils (chana dal, toor dal)
  • 1 cup grated bottle gourd (lauki)
  • 2 tablespoons yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-green chili paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 2 tablespoons oil

Instructions

  1. Soak rice and lentils for 4-5 hours, then grind into a batter.
  2. Mix grated bottle gourd, yogurt, ginger paste, turmeric, and salt.
  3. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, and pour into the batter.
  4. Pour batter into a greased pan, cover, and cook until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with green chutney.
