Punjabi Aloo Paratha

Flatbread stuffed with spiced potatoes, ideal for cozy mornings.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Servings: 2 people

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups wheat flour
  • 3 boiled potatoes, mashed
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions

  1. Prepare dough with wheat flour, salt, and water.
  2. Mix mashed potatoes with cumin, garam masala, and salt.
  3. Roll out dough, stuff with potato mixture, and seal.
  4. Cook on a hot tawa with ghee until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with yogurt and pickle.
