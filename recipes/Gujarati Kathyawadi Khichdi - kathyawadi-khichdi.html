Gujarati Kathyawadi Khichdi

A comforting mix of rice, lentils, and Gujarati spices.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 30 mins

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup split yellow moong dal
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice and moong dal together under running water.
  2. Heat ghee in a pressure cooker. Add cumin seeds and let them crackle.
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Stir in tomatoes and cook until soft. Add turmeric powder and salt.
  5. Add rice, moong dal, and water. Pressure cook for 3 whistles.
  6. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with ghee or pickle.
