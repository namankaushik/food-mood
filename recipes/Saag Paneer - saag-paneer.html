Saag Paneer

A creamy spinach dish paired with paneer cubes.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 20 mins

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 cups spinach, blanched and pureed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tablespoons cream
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee/oil in a pan and fry paneer cubes until golden. Set aside.
  2. In the same pan, add cumin seeds and chopped onions. Saut√© until golden.
  3. Add pureed tomatoes, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Stir in the spinach puree and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add cream and fried paneer cubes. Mix well and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with naan or rice.
