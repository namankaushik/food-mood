Punjabi Sarso Da Saag

A classic mustard greens dish, served with makki ki roti.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 40 mins

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sarso (mustard greens), cleaned and chopped
  • 1 cup spinach leaves, cleaned and chopped
  • 1/4 cup bathua (goosefoot), optional
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons makki ka atta (cornmeal)
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Clean and chop all the greens (mustard, spinach, and bathua).
  2. Pressure cook the greens with green chilies and a little salt for 2 whistles.
  3. Once cooled, mash the greens using a blender or wooden whisk.
  4. In a pan, heat ghee and add cumin seeds, ginger, and garlic. Saut√© until aromatic.
  5. Add the mashed greens and stir well. Sprinkle cornmeal to thicken and cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with a dollop of butter alongside makki ki roti.
