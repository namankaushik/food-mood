Gujarati Kadhi Recipe

A light and flavorful yogurt-based curry, perfect for any occasion.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 15 mins

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups plain yogurt (curd)
  • 3 tbsp gram flour (besan)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2 dried red chilies
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp ginger paste
  • 1/2 tsp green chili paste
  • A pinch of asafoetida (hing)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together yogurt, gram flour, turmeric powder, and water until smooth.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds; let them splutter.
  3. Add asafoetida, ginger paste, green chili paste, and dried red chilies. Saut√© for a few seconds.
  4. Slowly pour the yogurt mixture into the pan while stirring continuously to prevent curdling.
  5. Add salt to taste and simmer on low heat for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with steamed rice or khichdi.
