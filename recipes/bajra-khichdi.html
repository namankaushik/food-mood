Haryanvi Bajra Khichdi

Nutritious millet khichdi, perfect for healthy meals.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 25 mins

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup bajra (pearl millet), soaked for 2 hours
  • 1/2 cup moong dal
  • 2 tablespoons ghee
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups water
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the soaked bajra and moong dal in running water.
  2. Heat ghee in a pressure cooker and add cumin seeds.
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Stir in turmeric powder, salt, bajra, and moong dal.
  5. Pour 3 cups of water and mix everything well.
  6. Pressure cook for 4 whistles. Let the pressure release naturally.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with yogurt or pickle.
