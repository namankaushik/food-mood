Haldi Doodh

Golden milk infused with turmeric and spices.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 5 mins

Cook Time: 10 mins

Servings: 2 people

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon honey or sugar
  • Pinch of black pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat milk in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add turmeric powder, ginger, cinnamon, and black pepper.
  3. Whisk well and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat, add honey or sugar, and stir well.
  5. Serve warm and enjoy!
