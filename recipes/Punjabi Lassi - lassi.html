Punjabi Mast Lassi

A refreshing yogurt drink, served chilled.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 10 mins

Servings: 2 people

Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups yogurt
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts for garnish

Instructions

  1. Whisk yogurt, cold water, and sugar until smooth.
  2. Add cardamom powder and mix well.
  3. Pour into glasses, garnish with nuts, and serve chilled.
