Gujarati Dhokla

A light, steamed chickpea flour snack.

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 10 mins

Cook Time: 20 mins

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour
  • 1 tablespoon semolina
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Mix gram flour, semolina, turmeric, and salt with water to form a batter.
  2. Add ginger paste and lemon juice to the batter.
  3. Mix in baking soda just before steaming.
  4. Steam the batter in a greased tray for 20 minutes.
  5. Cut into pieces and garnish with coriander. Serve hot.
